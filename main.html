Summary

The Matrix is a 1999 science fiction film that depicts a dystopian future in which humanity is unknowingly trapped inside a simulated reality, the Matrix, created by sentient machines to subdue the human population. The film follows Neo, a computer hacker who learns about the true nature of his reality and his role in the war against its controllers.
